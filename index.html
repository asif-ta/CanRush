ABOUT

It all began in the late 1960s, in a small coastal town where the scent of the sea mixed with the sweetness of fresh fruit. A humble shop stood by the bustling old road, its wooden shelves lined with glass-bottled sodas from a time when every sip was a celebration. Travelers and locals alike would pause under the flickering glow of a single street lamp, sharing stories over cold, fizzy drinks.

But as time passed, the world changed. Glass bottles gave way to cans, and those nostalgic flavors faded into memories. Determined to revive that golden era, a new chapter began—a shop dedicated solely to canned soft drinks, bringing back the thrill of cracking open an ice-cold can, just like in the old days. Here, every drink tells a story. Some are classics from distant lands, while others are bold new flavors waiting to be discovered. Whether you’re reliving a memory or making a new one, this is more than just a shop—it’s a time capsule of taste, where every sip takes you back.

So step in, pick your favorite, and let the fizz take you on a journey through time.
